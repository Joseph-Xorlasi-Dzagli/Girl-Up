Our History

Mission Queens is a subsidiary club of girl up dedicated to securing equal opportunities for underprivileged adolescent girls in developing countries.

Our History

Mission Queens is a campaign of the United Nations Foundation

dedicated to securing equal opportunities for underprivileged

adolescent girls in developing countries. It was established 2019 by

Jacinta Dzagli, Sandra Acquah and Nana Oduraa Hewton. Mission Queens is

subsidiary club of Girl Up in Ghana and has been in operation since

September, 2019. It decided to adopt the name Mission Queens to set it

apart from other subsidiary clubs operating under in different countries.
